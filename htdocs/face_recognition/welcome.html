Page Description
Welcome Welcoming page that explains how to go through this system.
Notification To display LATE/EARLY/NO RECORD messages. Whenever an employee has taken an attendance, but he's late for more than 30 minutes, a LATE message will be displayed.
Report To display the analysis of the employee for the month.

Attendance column. Display attendance percentage, is affected by Late and Early column, but does not affected by No Record column. Formula: (Total Days - Late - Early) / Total days

Late column. Display how many times an employee is late for work. Late will only count employee who is late more than 30 minutes. Then, it will display the date that the employee is late on.

Early column. Display how many times an employee leaves early for break. For example, afternoon break time starts at 12:30pm, but the employee leaves for break at 12:20pm. Then the system will display the Early warning. Next, a date will be displayed to show which date the employee has gotten the Early warning.

No Record column. Display how many times an employee did not record their attendance.
Register Employee To register employee into system.

Employee ID. You can only enter numbers, recommended to keep it simple, such as ID = 1, ID = 2, ID = 3...

First Name. The First name of the employee.
Middle Name. The Middle name of the employee, can be NULL.
Last Name. The Last name of the employee, can be NULL.

Job Position. Choose from a list of job positions. They are Supervisor, Assistant Supervisor, Training Supervisor, EDP Clerk, and General Worker.

Registered employee can be seen in the List of Employee page.
Insert Timetable To register working days for the employee.
You must register some employee in Register Employee page first before continuing at this page.

Step 1. Choose a date by clicking on the Date Box.
Step 2. Choose an employee from the list by clicking the button. The number of employees it displays can be seen in the List of Employee page.
Step 3. Select shift (Morning, Afternoon, Full Day) for that date.
Step 4. Click Apply.

Registered data can be viewed in View Timetable page.
View Timetable To display which employee will be working on that particular date.

Employee will be displayed in this format (ID First Name Middle Name Last Name).
Change/Delete Timetable To change or delete an employee from the timetable. There are 2 tables, Change Timetable table and Delete Timetable table.

Change Timetable table.
Step 1. Select an employee on the particular date.
Step 2. Select new date (Can be the same date).
Step 3. Select new employee (Can be the same employee).
Step 4. Select new shift (Can be the same shift).
Step 5. Click Change button.
Note: Only dates after today's date is allowed to be changed.
View Attendance To view the recorded attendance for a particular employee on particular date.
The table consists of 5 timestamp, Morning, Afternoon Break, Afternoon, Evening Break, Evening, and End.
Employee who has Morning Shift will be using Morning (8AM-12:30PM), Afternoon Break (12:30PM-1:30PM), Afternoon (1:30PM-5PM), End (5PM) timestamp.
Employee who has Afternoon Shift will be using Afternoon (12:30-4PM, Evening Break (4PM-5PM), Evening (5PM-9L30PM), and End (9:30PM) timestamp.
Employee who has Full Day Shift will be using all the 5 timestamps. Morning (8AM-12:30PM), Afternoon Break (12:30PM-1:30PM), Afternoon (1:30PM-4PM), Evening Break (4PM-5PM), Evening (5PM-9:30PM), End (9:30PM)

Late indicates the employee recorded the attendance 30 minutes later than the stipulated time.
Early indicates the employee recorded the attendance early than the break time. For example, Afternoon breaks start at 12:30PM, but employee recorded attendance at 12:20PM.
No Record indicates employee did not record attendance on that particular timestamp.
Nil indicates that the particular timestamp does not belong to the shift. For example, Morning Shift will only use 4 timestamps, they are Morning, Afternoon Break, Afternoon, and End. Thus, Evening Break and Evening timestamp will display Nil as it not part of the Morning Shift.
List of Employees To display list of registered employee in the system.
Employee that has been registered through Register Employee page will be displayed here.
